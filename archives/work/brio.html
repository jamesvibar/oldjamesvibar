BRIO

Graphic Design

Brio is a fictional company and any associated companies with the same name are unintended.

The task was to create a logo of a construction company with the name 'Brio'. This was completed back in 2016 as a part of the final requirements for Desktop Publishing.

The Process

Going simple is really the best course of action when it comes to logos (well most of the time). Since a construction company is involved in building houses, I made the logo blocky and top it off with some vector buildings.
